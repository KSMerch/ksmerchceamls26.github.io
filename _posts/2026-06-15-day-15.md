what_i_learned: |
  Today was our first day transitioning from the online portion of the program to the in-person experience. We started with an orientation session that reintroduced us 
  to the expectations, schedule, and resources available throughout the remainder of the program. It was helpful to learn more about how the in-person portion will 
  operate and what we can expect in the coming weeks. After orientation, we attended a workshop on the foundations of data analytics. I learned about the role data 
  plays in research and decision-making, as well as how data can be collected, analyzed, and interpreted to answer questions. Since my project involves working with 
  ECG datasets and machine learning models, it was useful to see how data analytics connects to the research process. We also participated in an engineering-style 
  team challenge where groups competed to build the tallest tower using spaghetti, tape, and a marshmallow, with the marshmallow required to be at the very top. 
  The activity emphasized teamwork, communication, creativity, and problem-solving. Our group successfully built the tallest tower and finished in first place. 
  At the end of the day, we attended a research orientation where we were introduced to our lab locations and learned where we would be working for the rest of the 
  program. This helped make the transition to the in-person environment feel more organized and gave me a better sense of where our research activities will take place.

reflection: |
  Today was exciting because it marked the beginning of the in-person portion of the program. After spending so much time learning and collaborating online, it was 
  nice to finally meet people face-to-face and become more familiar with the environment where we will be working. My favorite part of the day was definitely the 
  spaghetti tower challenge. It was fun working together as a team to come up with a design, test ideas, and make adjustments along the way. Winning first place made 
  the activity even more enjoyable and showed how effective teamwork and communication can be when everyone contributes their ideas. I also enjoyed learning more about 
  data analytics because it connected to the type of work we have been doing in our project. Even though the workshop covered broad concepts, it helped me see how data 
  is at the center of both research and artificial intelligence. Overall, I left feeling excited, motivated, and ready to begin the next phase of the program in person.
