what_i_learned: |
  Today, I focused on creating our group's elevator pitch by helping film, act in, and edit the final video. Throughout the process, I learned more about how much 
  planning and coordination go into producing a short presentation. Even though an elevator pitch is only a brief summary of a project, it takes careful thought to 
  decide what information is most important and how to communicate it effectively. While filming, I learned the importance of speaking clearly, maintaining confidence, 
  and presenting information in a way that is engaging for the audience. Since our project involves technical concepts such as machine learning, ECG classification, 
  and fairness analysis, we had to think carefully about how to explain our research in a way that could be understood by people who may not have a technical 
  background. After filming, I spent the remainder of the day recording voiceovers and adding captions to the video. Through this process, I learned how these 
  elements can improve accessibility and make a presentation easier to follow. Recording voiceovers also reinforced the importance of pacing, pronunciation, and 
  clear communication. Adding captions required careful attention to detail to ensure the text accurately reflected the audio and synchronized properly with the 
  video. Overall, today helped me develop skills in communication, presentation, video production, and accessibility while continuing to share our research with a 
  broader audience.
